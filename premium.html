Turtl premium

The Turtl service is free and in most cases should work for most users. However, beginning soon, Turtl will launch a Premium service that will include more storage and will unlock various features in the Turtl apps.

In the meantime while we figure out what our Premium service will offer, please consider making a donation to aid in Turtl's development!

If you want the total freedom and security of Turtl without paying us, you are more than free to run your own Turtl server.
